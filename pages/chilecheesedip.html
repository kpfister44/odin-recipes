Chile Cheese Dip

Description

Have you ever needed a last minute dish to bring to a friends house for a get together? Or to watch a game with? Look no more, this simple chile cheese dip recipe is easy to make and delicious!

Make sure you have access to a crockpot for this recipe. You could make it in the microwave or oven, but it cooks much better in a crockpot.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of ground beef (85%-90% lean)
  • 1/2 block of Velveeta cheese
  • A bag of tortilla chips
  • 6 cans of Hormel Chile

Steps

  1. brown the ground beef in a pan and drain the access fat
  2. place the ground beef into a crockpot
  3. place 1/2 of the Velveeta block into the crockpot
  4. open and pour in the 6 cans of Hormel Chile into the crockpot
  5. cook on low for 5 hours
  6. enjoy with toritalla chips!
