Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

This is a simple to make buffalo chicken dip which will blow your socks off! All you need is a crockpot, hot sauce, butter, and some cream cheese to make a dip that will blow all your friends and family away. Simple and satisfying!

You can alter the top of hot sauce and cream cheese you used based on how spicy and fattening you want the dip. Also, greek yogurt can be substituted instead of cream cheese if you are looking for a more calorie friendly version of the dip.

Ingredients

  • 3lbs of chicken breast
  • 8oz of cream chesse (one standard package)
  • tortilla chips
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1/2 a bottle of Frank's Red Hot Sauce (standard size)

Steps

  1. add 3lbs of chicken breast to the crockpot
  2. add 1/2 stick of butter to the crockpot
  3. add 8oz package of cream cheese to the crockpot
  4. add 1/2 a bottle of Frank's Red Hot Sauce to the crockpot
  5. cook on high for 5-6 hours
  6. shred the chicken cooked with a wooden spoon and fork
  7. pour into a bowl and enjoy with your favorite tortilla chips!
